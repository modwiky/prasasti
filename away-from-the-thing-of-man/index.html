Three Wise Monkey

The common interpretation of the legend three wise monkey is

see no evil, hear no evil, and speak no evil

Sounds simple enough but I will say that it goes deeper than that. For me, it means that you must cover your eyes and ears so the world can have no influence on you. If you exposed enough to it you will become it and you have no choice but to speak like everybody else, in other words you become the world. and by-the-way that's not bad either.

You see, it's not really about what is evil. We can say see good, hear good, and speak good. and that's totally valid but what does it means?

Shifu: Master! Master! it's very bad news.
Master Oogway: There is just news, there is no good or bad.
Shifu: Master, your vision, your vision was right. Tai Lung has broken out of prison he's on his way
Master Oogway: That's bad news!

see no evil, hear no evil, and speak no evil

Perhaps the message behind this legend is not to give a maxim that people can follow but rather to tell us how the mind works.
